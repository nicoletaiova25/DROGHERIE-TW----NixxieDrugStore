### Nixxie DrugStore 🌿✨ - General Description 🍄

<p>Welcome to Nixxie DrugStore🌿✨, your one-stop shop for everything you need, beautifully designed in one place! 
  The site is a comprehensive platform developed for the Web Techniques course, utilizing JavaScript, Node.js, Bootstrap, JSON, HTML, CSS, and pgAdmin database. 
  We are excited to offer a feature-rich and user-friendly experience.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>
      <h2>Key Features and Functionalities 🌟</h2>
  </summary>
      <ul>
        <li><b>Homepage</b> 🏠: Our main page is packed with various sections, including a blog 📝, articles 📚, discounted products 🏷️, and the latest news 📰.</li>
        <li><b>Complex Menu</b> 🍔: Navigate through our user-friendly, comprehensive menu effortlessly.</li>
        <li><b>User Registration</b> ✍️: New users can easily register, receive a confirmation email 📧, and confirm their account through a provided link.</li>
        <li><b>User Profile Management</b> 👤: Users can log in 🔑, view and edit their profiles 🖊️, delete their accounts 🗑️, and log out.</li>
        <li><b>Product Page</b> 🛒: Explore our product page with advanced filtering options.</li>
        <li><b>Product Details</b> 📦: Clicking on any product redirects users to a detailed product page containing all relevant information.</li>
      </ul>
